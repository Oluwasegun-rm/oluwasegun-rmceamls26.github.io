what_i_learned: |
  Today was largely focused on research and project development within our lab groups. A significant portion of the day was spent working with my team to develop the research questions that will guide our project throughout the summer. Before creating these questions, we spent time reading and discussing materials related to our project in order to deepen our understanding of the problem we are trying to address. We carefully examined the problem statement, the motivations behind the research, and the contributions we hope our work will make.

  Through collaborative discussions with my teammates and graduate mentor, we brainstormed and refined potential research questions that were both meaningful and aligned with the goals of the project. This process helped me understand that strong research questions are critical because they shape the direction of the entire study and influence the methods, analysis, and outcomes of the research. I also spent time learning and discussing concepts that are directly related to our project, which helped me build a stronger foundation for the work we will be doing in the coming weeks.

  In addition to our research activities, I continued working on my website replication prototype in Figma. It was enjoyable to further develop the design and apply the user interface and design principles that we have been learning.
