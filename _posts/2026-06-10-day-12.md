what_i_learned: |
  Today’s activities focused on both the technical and research aspects of our project. We spent time examining the structure of the dataset, looking at the available columns and features, understanding the types of data they contained, and identifying any potential issues that may need to be addressed before further analysis. Looking closely at the dataset gave me a deeper understanding of the information available to us and helped our team think about how different features may contribute to our project moving forward.

We reviewed strategies for reading and understanding research papers, identifying key contributions, and recognizing how individual studies fit into the broader research landscape. We also discussed effective research writing practices, including how to develop strong background sections and present ideas in a clear and organized manner. These sessions have continued to strengthen my understanding of both the research process and the communication skills required to effectively share research findings.
