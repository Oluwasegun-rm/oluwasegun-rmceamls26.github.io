I experimented with different feature allocations and configurations to observe how they affected predictive performance. By adjusting the number and composition of features used by each modality, I was able to compare AUROC scores and better understand how feature selection influences the overall behavior of the model. These experiments helped me think more critically about balancing predictive performance while maintaining robustness across different biological data modalities.

  Overall, today's work combined opportunities for both communication and experimentation. Presenting our progress helped reinforce my understanding of the project, while the feature budgeting experiments provided additional insight into how different design choices can impact model performance.
