what_i_learned: |
  Today’s work focused on understanding the new research direction that our team has begun exploring and how it aligns with the overall goals of our project. A significant portion of the day was spent discussing the research pivot with my teammates, professor, and graduate mentor to better understand the motivation behind the shift and the opportunities it presents. These discussions helped me gain a clearer understanding of the problem we are trying to solve and how our project may evolve moving forward.

  As part of this process, I spent time conducting literature reviews and examining related research papers to understand how other researchers have approached similar problems. Reviewing these papers allowed me to identify common methodologies, datasets, and evaluation techniques that have been used in previous studies. It was valuable to see how existing research can guide project development and help researchers make informed decisions when exploring new directions.

  I also continued exploring datasets related to Major Depressive Disorder (MDD), particularly datasets containing genetic and biological information that may be relevant to our research objectives. Using Python and Google Colab, I loaded and examined several datasets to better understand their structure, available features, and overall suitability for our project. This involved inspecting variables, reviewing data formats, and evaluating whether the datasets contained the information necessary to support our research goals.

  In addition, I continued developing my data cleaning and preprocessing skills while working with these datasets. Handling different file formats, exploring feature sets, and assessing data quality gave me a greater appreciation for the amount of preparation required before meaningful analysis can begin. Overall, today’s activities helped me better understand our project’s evolving direction while strengthening my skills in literature review, dataset evaluation, and data preparation.

blockers: |
One challenge I encountered today involved working with a .tar file format, which I had not used before. Initially, I experienced difficulty determining how to properly extract and load the contents into Python for analysis. This slowed down my workflow for a period of time, but after conducting some research and experimenting with different approaches, I was able to understand the format and successfully access the data. The experience reinforced the importance of persistence and problem-solving when working with unfamiliar data sources.

reflection: |
  Today helped me better understand how research projects evolve over time. As our team continues to refine its direction, I am beginning to appreciate the importance of remaining flexible and willing to explore new approaches when necessary. The discussions surrounding our research pivot showed me that research is often an iterative process that requires continuous learning, evaluation, and adaptation.

  I also found value in reviewing related research papers and seeing how other researchers have approached similar problems. This process helped me understand that many of the challenges we encounter have been explored before, and that existing literature can provide useful guidance when developing new ideas. Looking at how other teams structured their methodologies, selected datasets, and evaluated their results gave me a better understanding of how research is conducted in practice.

  In addition, working with datasets and evaluating their suitability for our project reinforced the idea that the quality of a research project is heavily influenced by the quality of the data being used. Overall, today’s activities strengthened my understanding of both the research process and the technical work involved in preparing data for analysis. I left the day with a clearer understanding of our project’s direction and greater confidence in my ability to navigate unfamiliar concepts, datasets, and technical challenges.
